About US

PRIMEPOINT PARTNERS IS AN EXPERT SOURCING PLATFORM FOR PROJECT IMPLEMENTATION AND CORPORATE ADVISORY IN THE WESTERN BALKANS. WE WORK WITH THE BEST EXPERTS TO DELIVER OUTSTANDING RESULTS TO OUR CLIENTS AND MAKE AN IMPACT IN THE REGION.

PrimePoint Partners is a private-partnership consulting company based in Macedonia, that provides sourcing services for deploying experts to projects in Macedonia and the region. We provide professional project implementation and corporate advisory services by combining a team of the most reliable and most relevant experts. We are affiliated with an extensive number of regional and international consultants with expertise in a variety of sectors including education and training, IT, technology commercialization, natural resources, agri-business, tourism, textile, financial and legal services, energy and renewable energy, etc. Our clients are private companies, international development organizations, municipalities, NGOs and foreign investors. We are passionate about developing the best community of experts for the Western Balkan region so we can bring value to our clients through the quality of implementation, trustworthiness and impact-oriented approach. PrimePoint Partners is focused on brain gain – most of our experts belong to the international expat community and are eager to contribute to the development of the region.

Lou Naumovski

Mr. Naumovski is a Non-Executive Director, Amur Minerals Corporation and Trans-Siberian Gold operating in Russia and is Senior Advisor to McCain Foods and BRP of Canada and Seequent (New Zealand). Mr. Naumovski began his career as a diplomat and trade commissioner for Canada (1980-1990). His assignments included Moscow, Atlanta, Georgia, Baghdad, Iraq, and Deputy Director for Trade and Investment Development, Eastern Europe and the USSR in Ottawa. From 1990-1992 Mr. Naumovski was Executive Director of the Canada – USSR Business Council (CUBC). Mr. Naumovski is a volunteer member of the Board of Directors of the Canadian Executive Services Organization (CESO) and a member of the Board of Experts (and former Director) of Macedonia 2025, a non-profit international association of Macedonians in the Diaspora dedicated to supporting the economic and social development of the Republic of Macedonia. Mr. Naumovski holds a Hons. B.A in economics and political science (University of Toronto), and a M.A in international relations and Russian / Soviet affairs (Carleton University). Mr. Naumovski was a long-time member of the local Canada Eurasia Russia Business Association (CERBA) Toronto and Moscow Chapter Boards and was National Chairman of the association from 2013-2017. In 2016, Mr. Naumovski was awarded the Order of International Cooperation by Russian Foreign Minister Sergei Lavrov.

Our focus

Development Programs

We partner with international development organizations that implement projects for job creation, leadership and entrepreneurship development, women empowerment and equality, social inclusion, rural growth and tourism, youth engagement, etc. We provide capacity building to NGOs and municipalities in creating development strategies and getting access to funding.

Corporate Advisory

We work with local companies and we assist them with business strategy, strategic marketing, market research, financial advisory, investment planning, access to funding, access to new markets, technology commercialization and operations management. We assist companies with donor applications and project implementation.

Investments

We assist international investors or investment funds that consider investing in Macedonia or have already established a company, with feasibility studies, regulatory requirements and availability of government incentives, talent recruitment, linkages with local suppliers, etc.

Leadership Programs

We have developed a “Leadership Acceleration Program” that we deliver to companies for their management and team leaders. We also provide leadership training to start-up companies, students and NGOs. We provide strategic advisory on corporate leadership events, workshops, conferences and forums.

Values

TRANSFORM: We are passionate about projects that have the power to transform, drive change and growth for all.
QUALITY: We consider quality content and establishing efficient processes as essential in delivering sustainable results.
RESULT ORIENTED: We focus on delivering results with significant impact and we take an entrepreneurial approach towards all activities that we implement.
VALUES-BASED: We believe in values-based leadership and we strive to empower individuals to grow by listening, learning and leading them.
